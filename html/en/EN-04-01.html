Principles of Social Responsibility

principle

Doosan E&C, to strongly practice corporate social responsibility
management based on its own corporate philosophy, has
established and operates a corporate social responsibility
strategy system.

  • 01

    Doosan E&C's business strategy is focused on striving to meet and contribute to the goals of our society and of individuals.

  • 02

    Doosan E&C will continue to expand its positive impact on the environment and society through its management activities, while striving to reduce negative impacts and manage any risks.

  • 03

    Doosan E&C encourages sustainable business practices and enables economic activities for the shared prosperity of present and future generations, by working together responsibly with both clients and partners.

  • 04

    Doosan E&C will proactively and responsibly communicate and cooperate with the relevant stakeholders, to achieve its sustainable development goals.

  • 05

    Doosan E&C incorporates these principles in its governance and corporate culture, while reflecting them in its management activities.

  • 06

    Doosan E&C will monitor the implementation of these principles with the aim to transparently manage the positive and negative impacts of its management activities on our society.
