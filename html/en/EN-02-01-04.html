03 SMART GREEN SYSTEM

: Preventing the discomfort of living
(water leak) by applying the efficient
architecture hybrid water-proofing systems

For artificial ground greening integrated with soft FRP coating material and the inner sheat (non-woven fabric + PET film + self-adhesive butyl rubber) is a sheet-coating double composite waterproofingroot resistance method. Predicting the Formwork Demolding Time

04 Predicting the Formwork Demolding Time

: Moving up the removal time of the forms in
winter and improving the construction quality

In carrying out concrete construction during the winter season, this is a technology that can not only move up the formwork demolding time, but also it can make the prediction of the time possible according to the environmental conditions of each site and the ready-mixed concrete proportion conditions. Doosan E&C can effectively predict and manage the formwork demolding time, after calculating the maturity index of the concrete in consideration of the hydration heat generated from hardened concrete, which is a new concept of predicting the formwork demolding time by the maturity index-based method.

  • Application cases :  Administration Town Central Doosan We’ve, Doosan We’ve The Zenith Harbour City, Doosan We’ve The Zenith Central Saha

Implementation of Concrete Unit Quantity Measurement Standard

05 Implementation of Concrete Unit Quantity Measurement Standard

: Preemptive activities being carried out to
secure quality control

The unit water quantity of concrete formulation is important for concrete quality control as a factor that has a great influence on not only the constructability of unhardened concrete, but also the compressive strength, shrinkage cracks and durability of hardened concrete. Recently, as the concrete unit water quantity measurement test has been applied to strengthen ready-mixed concrete quality control, preemptive activities are being carried out to secure quality control at all sites.
