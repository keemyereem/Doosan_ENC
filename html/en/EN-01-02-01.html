Daegu Doosan We’ve the Zenith

Daegu Doosan We’ve the Zenith is the first mega-scale residential-commercial complex built in the Daegu region, and is an innovative residential building that redefines the residential culture of Daegu, or the domestic residential culture. Despite in December, off-season, apartment distribution has been successfully completed in a short period of time, surprising the press and the market, and this housing project is one in which Doosan E&C secured competitiveness in the high-rise market by applying a number of new technologies and construction methods. In November 2008, it won the Minister of Land, Infrastructure and Maritime Affairs' Award at the Construction Environment Management Best Practices Competition; in October 2009 it was honored with the Presidential Award for Safety of the Republic of Korea.
