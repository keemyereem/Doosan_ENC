Management Philosophy

  • Advancing toward
    a better world,
    by building
    a better tomorrow!

    Since it was founded in 1960, Doosan E&C has been developing its capabilities and growing alongside the Korean construction industry, by accumulating outstanding achievements in architecture, housing, and civil works,
    based on a solid foundation of abundant experience and remarkable technologies.
    Doosan E&C has embarked on a journey to reach its ambitious goal to be a solid, long-lasting business,
    while strengthening its existing base and exploring business opportunities for sustainable growth.
    In addition, by staying ahead of our competition using innovative methods, we will create spaces with an up-scaled value.
    We will continuously put our heart and soul into bringing about change and innovation for a better future.

  • Core Principles of Business Operations

    • Establishment of Transparent Management

      Doosan E&C is growing together with its employees,
      by establishing and setting a high level of transparent managementas a strong driving force; accordingly, the company’s employees are fairly evaluated and rewarded for their efforts.

    • Reliable Quality and Brand

      Doosan E&C employees strive to meet the needs of their customers, as a trusted brand known for its high-quality results.
