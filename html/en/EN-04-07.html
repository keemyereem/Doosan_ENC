Leveraging the Business Competencies

Doosan E&C utilizes our business skills, to carry out
various activities and to practice sharing with our neighbors.

Habitat

Starting with the Unification Village in Paju, Gyeonggi Province, a total of 127 “houses of love” were built in 2008 to provide a more comfortable environment in neighborhoods suffering from poor housing conditions. This volunteer activity benefits from the participation of our executives and employees, but also by members of the Doowoo Association, who are a group of excellent partners.

Green City Cultivation

In order to enrich the city and the daily lives of residents, flower paths are being created in parks and other major recreation sites. Together with local governments and community volunteer centers, we select a place that will benefit from the project, plant seasonal flowers and shrubs that blend in with the local environment, and then paint the facilities together.

Supporting the Community

Doosan E&C strives to deliver a comfortable environment in local communities;
furthermore, we express our gratitude for the heroes who are making sacrifices day and
night to ensure that we can live in a safe environment.

On-Site Sharing

At each site, there are activities to improve residential facilities, such as the renovation of old houses in the vicinity, Hope Ondol warm winter projects, and support for flood-damaged households. Through various programs, such as delivering safety items to fire-vulnerable households to help them prepare for social disasters, we are striving to support the development and co-existence of local communities.

Sharing Tea of Love

Since 1992, we have visited military bases through our “Tea of Love” program, to boost morale and express our gratitude for the hard work of military officers and enlisted men who are performing their national defense duties.

Activating Executive and Employee Engagement

The Doosan E&C executives and employees voluntarily donated gifts to the Social Welfare Joint Fundraising Association,
so that socially disadvantaged people would be supported through various projects, such as
housing environment improvement projects with welfare institutions for the disabled, medical support projects for
the underprivileged, support projects to provide daily necessities for the vulnerable, and so on.
