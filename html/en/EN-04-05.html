Win-Win Partnership

Doosan E&C is building a win-win management system, based on virtuous cycle partnerships with
its partners, as its philosophy of ensuring shared growth.

Doosan E&C goes beyond the existing structure of simple purchase/subcontract contracts, and
provides joint technology developments with it partner companies, as well as construction VE (Value Engineering) performance sharing, financial support to strengthen liquidity, and educational programs. In addition, we have established a “virtuous cycle partnership” to further strengthen our relationships with
our partner companies through compliance with fair trade. Doosan E&C strives to promote a shared growth strategy at the
company-wide level, and is committed to developing a detailed action plan along with the company’s business plan.
We operate a systematic monitoring system, which is reflected in the performance evaluations of our managers.

Virtuous cycle partnership

Fair selection and evaluation of partner companies

Doosan E&C uses “Partner Company Selection and Operation Guidelines” to select and manage a registered company accordingly.
Based on the results of performance evaluations every year, excellent suppliers are selected, while benefits such as exemptions and
the reduction of contract performance guarantees are provided.
