Doosan E&C Environmental Management Policy

Doosan E&C recognizes the realization of humanistic management practices as a core means of
environmental management, and in the entire process related to our products and services,
we continuously look to improve any environment that is harmful to our executives, employees,
workers and stakeholders, while considering all the risk factors.

  • 01

    Compliance with environmental laws and other requirements

    We comply with environmental laws, agreements, and other requirements related to our business activities, and we establish strict in-house management standards that are faithfully implemented.

  • 02

    Pollution prevention and environmental protection

    We develop eco-friendly technologies and products to prevent environmental pollution, and practice activities designed to reduce emissions of greenhouse gases and pollutants.

  • 03

    Communication with stakeholders

    We communicate with regard to our environmental management performance with internal/external stakeholders honestly and transparently, in order to fulfill our responsibilities to the local community.

  • 04

    Operation of an Environmental Management System

    We establish, operate, and continuously improve and develop a management system designed to improve the environmental impact generated by our products, activities, and services.

  • 05

    Practice of an Environmental Management System

    The company’s Environmental Management Policy is firmly recognized and practiced by all of our executives, employees and partners, without exception.

Key Environmental Management Measures

Doosan E&C has introduced and operates an Environmental Management System for an eco-friendly construction site.
We have strengthened the best self-monitoring system for all stages, from a site’s opening to its completion.

  • Initial site SET-UP support

    a supervising system for carrying out the legal on-site management of the initial construction environment

  • On-site environmental
    management status assessment

    To enhance the on-site environmental management performance and proactively respond to environmental accidents, we are conducting full-scale inspections on all sites.

  • Enhancement of
    environment manager competence

    Excellent environment managers are nurtured through providing the environment managers with diverse and practical training and information.

Environmental Management System Certificate

  • ISO 14001

    As Doosan E&C is committed to ensuring the effectiveness and efficiency of its system, and in order to establish an optimal Environmental Management System, we are committed to constantly reviewing and improving it. In addition, to improve the level of our environmental management system so that it is consistent and appropriate, we establish environmental measures in line with the latest laws and regulations, as well as on-site environmental goals, detailed aims and promotion plans, to comply with the ISO 14001 system and to incorporate and operate environmental management in the field.
