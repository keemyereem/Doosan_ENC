CEO Message

“ Based on good technologies, high-quality,
and a customer-centered management strategy,
we are building a better standard of living for you. ”

Doosan E&C boasts of a 60-year history and tradition.
It has successfully carried forward a number of monumental projects in the Korean
construction field, and has grown to be a leading company in the industry.
As a result, Doosan E&C, riding on the back of the “Doosan We’ve” brand power
that is leading the nation’s housing culture, is showing its outstanding
competitiveness in urban renewal (redevelopment and reconstruction) Projects.
The company is also elevating its standing in urban development projects
displayed through supersized buildings in major cities, including the capital area.

Doosan Engineering & Construction, which also excelled in the civil engineering business,
the first privately proposed railway in Korea, and carried out route planning, construction, and operation all at once,
recording the longest extension construction performance in Korea in the high-speed rail sector,
we greatly contributed to infrastructure construction and national economic development.

In addition, Doosan E&C has newly organized the Safety & Health Office
to strongly secure ‘safety and health,’ which has emerged as the primary value in business activity;
the Chief Safety Officer is leading this office systematically to secure safety with high-quality execution.

As a corporation focused on fulfilling the goal of customer satisfaction,
Doosan E&C has endeavored to provide all its customers with the highest-level technologies and quality,
as well as the best service. In the future, we will continuously exert all our efforts
by using our management principle of “Transparent Management,”
so that we will become a corporation that grows along with society,
practices the customer-first principle, and fulfills our corporation social responsibility.

Thank you.

Chairman and CEO, Doosan E&C

Kwon Kyoung Hoon
