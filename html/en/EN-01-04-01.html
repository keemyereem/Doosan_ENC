Gyeongbu High-speed Railway

The Gyeongbu High-speed Railway, called the transportation revolution of the 21st century, was the greatest national project to take place since ancient times. Seoul Metro Line 9 (Sector 909)

Seoul Metro Line 9 (Sector 909) was the site of the one of the only three large-diameter shield tunnels to be built in Korea at the time of its construction. This is the third shield construction attempted by Doosan E&C, and the first shield construction in Seoul Subway; for the first was the EPB-type shield construction for the Gwangju Subway Sector 1-1 site, and the second was the slurry-type shield construction for the underwater tunnel site of Busan Subway Sector 230. Gangnam Beltway (Sector 5)

The Gangnam Beltway Expressway runs from Doksan-dong, Geumcheon-gu, Seoul to Suseo-dong, Gangnam-gu, with a total length of 22.9 km. As an urban highway, the width of the road is 6~8 lanes, including 3 tunnels, 2 business offices, and 2 ICs. Of the total sections, 12.4km spanning Geumcheon-gu, Gwanak-gu, and Seocho-gu is private- invested, with a total project cost of KRW 2.4612 trillion (including KRW 909.8 billion in private investment portion). In addition, to minimize damage to nature, tunnels were dug, spanning as long as 11 kilometers of the total extension. Section 5 of 5.1 km in length, which Doosan E&C is in charge of, spans from the Geumcheon Business Office in Siheung-dong, Geumcheon-gu to Gwanak IC, one Gwanak Tunnel and to one 14-lane Geumcheon Business Office. Dongdaegu Station Elevated Bridge

The total length of the Dongdaegu Station Elevated Bridge is 0.565 km, which is located around the entire area of Dongdaegu Station in Sinam-dong and Sincheon-dong, Dong-gu, Daegu-si. The elevated bridge is 338 meters long and the access road is 227meters. The bridge is a civil structure that connects the Gyeongbu High-speed Railway Phase 2 opening and the transfer center and the surroundings of the station; and it also has symbolism as a gateway to Daegu City. The safety construction that minimized train operation restrictions and traffic control was highly praised, and it won the Gold Prize at the '2018 Civil Structure of the Year Competition.’ Major Achievements in
