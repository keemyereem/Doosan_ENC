Safety and Health Management

Doosan E&C, recognizing safety and health as its top priority value and responsibility,
practices continuous managerial improvements to protect the life and safety of all employees based on
its humanistic safety culture and strong execution of accident prevention safety and technical safety.

Safety and Health Management Policy

Doosan E&C is operating a safety and health management system to effectively realize the safety and
health management policy, and encourages the participation of the field workers, partner companies,
and stakeholders, as well as the executives and employees, so that the system can be operated
more effectively to establish a safety culture.

  • 01

    Practice obligations to ensure safety and health

    All members of the company will pursue continuous measures in relation to the safety and health performance by improving unsafe working environments and eliminating unsafe behaviors, through education and the implementation of procedures.

  • 02

    Compliance with safety and health laws and other requirements

    We comply with safety and health laws, conventions, and other requirements related to our business activities, and we set strict internal management standards that are faithfully implemented.

  • 03

    Encourage autonomous participation of workers in safety policies

    We disseminate safety and health management policies to our workers and induce voluntary participation, through active communication and transparent management performance disclosures.

  • 04

    Cooperation in safety and health activities with our partners

    We expand our collaborations to promote the safety and health of stakeholders, such as our partner companies, and encourage the related companies to participate in safety and health activities.

  • 05

    Operation of an occupational safety and health management system

    We regularly evaluate whether the goals and activity plans established for the effective realization of our safety and health management policy have been understood, so that a safety culture can be established.
