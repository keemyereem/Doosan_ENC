History

Since its foundation in 1960, Doosan E&C has had the goal of becoming a sustainable company lasting a century,
based on the strength of its brilliant history over the past 60 years and its outstanding technology.
For many years to come, we will continue carrying out changes and innovations, as we advance toward a wider world and a brighter future.
